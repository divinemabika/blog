Fantasy Writing

There I stand in the darkest place of life. My life being consumed by the edited, overproduced and photoshopped world. With nowhere to go and no one to speak to I have fallen into the pits of hell. Every night I cry for the light, I wish for the light but it is nowhere to be seen. All I hear are the anguish cries of the canaries, women and children screaming for help, seeing men fighting pointless battle. There’s no way out, I'm lost.

My heart can’t take no more.

I want to get out. I need to escape this world, it sallowing every part of me, it stealing my energy, it consuming me, it make me angry, it makes me violent, but all I want is peace. I’m tired!
